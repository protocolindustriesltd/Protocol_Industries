Pioneering Progress Through Precision Engineering

Protocol Industries was founded by a trio of engineers with a shared passion for solving complex challenges. We thrive in the world of well-designed electronics to transform industries and improve lives. Our foundation is built on the principles of innovation, meticulous precision, and a commitment to education. We offer a comprehensive suite of services, from smart PCB design that optimizes for performance and manufacturability, to end-to-end custom electronics product development that brings ideas from a napkin sketch to a market-ready device.

Beyond our client work, we're dedicated to empowering the next generation of engineers through specialized training programs. Our passion is turning abstract concepts into high-performance electronic solutions that are both robust and reliable. We strive on collaboration, working closely with our clients to become a true extension of their team.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional custom electronics and PCB design solutions that exceed expectations. We are committed to fostering innovation by solving with rigorous engineering discipline, ensuring every project is executed with precision and purpose. We are committed to fostering the next generation of engineers, sharing knowledge and empowering others within our team and throughout the broader engineering community.

Our Vision

To be a leading force and a trusted partner in the electronics industry, recognized for our innovative designs, reliable product development, and high-quality training programs. We envision a future where technology is more accessible and powerful, and we strive to be at the forefront of that movement by developing cutting-edge solutions that are both practical and transformative worldwide through premier educational programs.
