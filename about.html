Our Story

Founded by three passionate engineers, Protocol Industries emerged from a shared vision to bridge the gap between innovative ideas and market-ready electronics. We recognized that many brilliant concepts fail to materialize due to the complexity of hardware development and the scarcity of experienced PCB designers.

Today, we combine our expertise in circuit design, embedded systems, and technical education to empower businesses and individuals to bring their electronic visions to life.
